# South America

## Salta dawn

Bus pulled into Salta about 5 in the morning. The sunrise over the Andes was
quiet — colour bleeding into the mountains one ridge at a time, not dramatic
at all. Felt like the kind of memory that outlasts the loud ones.

## Atacama silence

Two hours outside San Pedro the sky turned the colour of a bruise and you
could hear nothing — not even your own footsteps once you stopped walking.
The desert is the only place I've ever heard real silence.
