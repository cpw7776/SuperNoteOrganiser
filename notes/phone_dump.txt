Sex should be something sacred, something not easily attained, to keep it special in a relationship. Perhaps instead of having sex whenever each of you want, you limit it to a time and even place (except when trying for a baby). This would help you look forward to the act, to know this was the only time you were able to caress the body of your partner — you would spend more time to make it last and both were satisfied.

Got off the bus in Salta around 5am. The dawn over the Andes wasn't dramatic, just slow — colour seeping into the cordillera one ridge at a time. I think I'll remember it longer than the louder things on this trip.

Anything you do every day stops being a treat. Coffee, music, even sex. The mind discounts what it can't run out of. So engineering scarcity into pleasures is one of the few free upgrades available to a life — and almost nobody takes it.
